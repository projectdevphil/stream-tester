About Stream Tester

info About Us

Stream Tester is a simple, user-friendly tool designed for developers and streaming enthusiasts to test and debug HLS and MPEG-DASH video streams. It leverages the robustness of Shaka Player to provide a seamless playback experience, complete with support for various manifest formats and DRM technologies like Widevine and ClearKey.

Whether you're working with custom M3U playlists or need to manually input stream URIs, this tool offers the flexibility and detailed control necessary for effective stream validation.
